👋  Hello, I'm Rana, a PhD student at the University of Toronto, currently immersed in the world of metabolic pathways at the Laboratory for Metaoblic Systems Engineering. 

🧬 My research focuses on the intersection of deep learning and metablic engineering applications. Specifically, I study the intricacies of improving genome-scale modeling and computational protein engineering through deep learning. I like to think about it as exploring how cutting-edge AI technology can revolutionize our approach to understanding and manipulating biological systems at the metabolic scale. 

🔬 Excited about the endless possibilities at the intersection of biology and ML, I'm always open to collaborating with others in this space as well as mentoring students who are interested in learning more! Feel free to connect with me through email, LinkedIn, or X if you'd like to chat about the potential of ML in metabolic systems!
